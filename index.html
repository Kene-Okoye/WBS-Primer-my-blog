The secret of success in life is to eat what you like and let the food fight it out inside.

Mark Twain

As a kid, there was nothing more that made me happier than the expetation of a plate of well prepared meal at dinner. Egusi soup, bitterleaf soup, jollof rice (and I mean the original Nigerian jollof), fried rice, beans, ukwa, pounded yam etc. Having discovered the "secret of success life" - thanks to Mark Twain, I would like to engage you food lovers like myself with some of the best made in Nigeria recipes.
